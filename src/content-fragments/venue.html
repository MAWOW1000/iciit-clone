Swinburne Vietnam is an international education program between Swinburne University of Technology in Australia and FPT University, offering Swinburne University of Technology degree in Vietnam.
Swinburne University of Technology is a prestigious university ranked 285th and belongs to the top 1% of the top universities in the world (QS Ranking, 2024), with a history of more than 100 years since its establishment. In Vietnam, Swinburne University of Technology has been known for more than 20 years as a sponsor for the champions of the game show “Road to Olympia” with study opportunities in Australia.
FPT University is also one of the famous universities in Vietnam that has achieved many international certifications, such as QS Stars 3 stars (2012) and ACBSP for Business (2019). FPT University currently has locations in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang, Can Tho, and Quy Nhon.
All content, materials and quality assurance are delivered directly from Swinburne University of Technology (Melbourne, Australia). The academic lecturers delivered the courses all meet the international standard requirements of Swinburne University of Technology. Students’ undergraduate degrees upon graduation will be awarded by Swinburne University of Technology as students studying in Australia.
