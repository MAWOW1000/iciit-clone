Important Dates

2026 11th International Conference on Intelligent Information Technology (ICIIT 2026) aims to gather professors, researchers, scholars and industrial pioneers all over the world. ICIIT is the premier forum for the presentation and exchange of past experiences and new advances and research results in the field of theoretical and industrial experience. The conference welcomes contributions which promote the exchange of ideas and rational discourse between educators and researchers all over the world. We aim to building an idea-trading platform for the purpose of encouraging researcher participating in this event.  

Submission Deadline (Full paper) 10/01/2026
Submission Deadline (Abstract) 15/01/2026
Notification Date (Full paper or Abstract) 25/01/2026
Registration Deadline 30/01/2026
Conference Dates 05-08/03/2026

 

*Sumission Deadline: If you want to publish paper with presentation, full paper should be submitted via Electonic Submission System or conference email box: iciit@cbees.net before this date. If you just want to do presentation without paper submission, abstract can be submitted via Electonic Submission System or conference email box: iciit@cbees.net.

*Notification Date: After receiving your paper submission, paper will be sent to be peer reviewed by 2-3 reviewers or technical committee. Review result will be told on or before this date.

*Registration Deadline: If the full paper or abstract is accepted to be presented in the conference, registration documents including final full paper or final abstract, registration form, copyright form and payment proof should be uploaded in the online registration system or sent to the conference email box: iciit@cbees.net.

*Conference Dates: Conference will be held during this date. The academic visit will be arranged on March 08, 2026 and it is optional to join in.
