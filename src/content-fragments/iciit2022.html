About ICIIT 2022

 

2022 International Conference on Intelligent Information Technology (ICIIT 2022)  has been held successfully in Foshan, China (Online+Offline) during February 25-27, 2022.

Publication: ACM Conference Proceedings (ISBN: ISBN: 978-1-4503-9617-2), indexed by Ei Compendex and Scopus

Onsite Photo

Screenshot of Participants

During the conference, many conference organizing committee members participated in the Opening Ceremony. The General Chair of ICIIT 2022-Prof. Shaoming Luo from Foshan University, the Secretary of the Party Committee of Foshan University made an Opening Remarks. Followed by three great keynote speeches and two Invited Speeches.

The keynote speeches were carried by Prof. Rui Zhang, IEEE Fellow, from National University of Singapore. Prof. Edmund Lai, IET Fellow, from Auckland University of Technology, and Prof. Zhihua Wang, IEEE Fellow, from Tsinghua University.

Three oral sessions on “Algorithm Design and Computation”, “Intelligent Image Processing Technology and Application”, and “Electronic Systems and Information Engineering” were successfully held under the joint efforts of session chairs and all presenters. Experts and scholars from different countries and regions shared and reported their research results, and the session chairs selected 3 best oral presentations.


Optimal Resource Allocation of Mobile Edge Computing Using Grasshopper Optimization Algorithm
Zhonghua Li and Guannan He Sun
Yat-sen University, China

Oral Cone Beam Computed Tomography Images Segmentation Based On Multi-view Fusion
Yunyao Jin, Qijun Zhao and Junxin Cheng
Sichuan University, China

Multi-Scale Model for Growth of Tissues Regulated by Mechanical Loading
Qiyang Fan and Bin Chen
Zhejiang University, China
